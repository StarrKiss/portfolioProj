ABOUT US
The Azure Ocean Rail Union is the national railway provider of the AO. With over 38,000 kilometers of track, we are one of the backbones of transport and logistics, providing both passenger and freight services. The Union was founded over 100 years ago as a labor union of rail workers under the then-national rail company of the Lafarvets, before becoming the sole overseer of Azure Ocean rail during the worker's revolution. Since then, we have expanded our rail network to every corner of the AO, and established high-speed services travelling as fast as 150 miles per hour. For more on our history, visit our History page. For information on our freight services, please visit our freight rail website.
